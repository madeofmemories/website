hi, i'm sara, i like to read. in particular, i like books with:
  • good characters
  • immersive worldbuilding
  • absolutely no dumb love triangles and stupid protagonists

if these feelings are mutual, it seems that you may have found a literature treasure trove.
if not. . . this treasure trove may seem more like a dumpster fire.




some of my favorite books are: The Song of Ice and Fire series, Six of Crows series, The Raven Boys series, The Song of Achilles, Nevernight series, Jade City, and The Lies of Locke Lamora.
my favorite classics are: East of Eden and Pride and Prejudice.

in my free time, i play video games, watch tv, and listen to music.
they all influence the type of stories i enjoy and the characters i love.
